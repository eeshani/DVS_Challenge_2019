Freelancers in Data Visualization

This data visualization project is created in response to the DVS Challenge 2019 conducted by the Data Visualization Society focusing on the results of the Annual Data Visualization Community Survey. The Annual Data Visualization Community Survey for 2019 has responses to 50+ questions and was taken by over 1,350 people connected to the field of data visualization. This project is focussed specially on the responses of the freelancers in this field. Thus, it will help contributors understand the trends and technologies relevant to data visualization!
